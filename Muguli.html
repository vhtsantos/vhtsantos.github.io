Muguli VR

Developer, Modeler

Short Description

This project is a VR Game combined with a website that was created with the goal of partnering with Vista Alegre, a porcelain factory that also has a museum that showcases some of its historic features and pieces. In the game, you'll be able to model your own porcelain pieces going through every step of their creation, while having an interactive virtual museum.
I was responsible for modeling its structure and most of the 3D models and, on the development state in unity, for programming the steps of the pottery creation. The pottery pieces made by the players are sent directly to the game's website through a database and displayed on the player's creations webpage. In this particular project, I and my group gained a lot of experience dealing with different areas. Every single member of our group specialized in different subjects and we managed to come together to assemble the game with a database and a website. This was particularly valued by our advisors who then awarded us with a really good grade on the final prototype (18/20).
