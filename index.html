Who Am I?

I'm a 3D Artist and Game Developer based in Porto, Portugal. I have a bachelor's degree in Multimedia and Communication Technologies and am currently taking my master's degree in Multimedia - Interactive Technologies and Digital Games at Faculdade de Engenharia da Universidade do Porto

I've always loved everything around 3D. From creating YouTube banners with 3D renders and selling them as a freelancer, when I was a teen, to now making short films, animations, and games. It has been a passion of mine since forever. In fact, I've always been a bit ahead of my peers because I've been eager to learn and explore more. I'm an athletic and active person and I love chasing good aesthetics and clean visuals in both my work and life.

I have dedicated approximately two years of my time to 3D, which has been the core of my experience. I applied my short experience in a couple of personal, indie and academic projects, and even won an award with my recent short animation "Scrap Heads: The Clock is Ticking".

Experienced in Blender, Substance Painter, Zbrush, Unreal Engine and Unity, and currently on the learning process of Autodesk Maya.
