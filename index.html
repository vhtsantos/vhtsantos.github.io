Who Am I?

I am a 3D and XR Developer, 3D Generalist and Game Developer with videography experience, based in Porto, Portugal. I hold a Master's degree in Multimedia – Interactive Technologies and Digital Games from the Faculty of Engineering of the University of Porto (final grade 17.4/20) and a Bachelor's degree in Multimedia and Communication Technologies.

I have three years of experience in the production of XR applications, 3D applications and games, as well as audiovisual content, including videography and drone operation.

The software I use most frequently includes Unity3D, Unreal Engine, Blender, ZBrush, Substance Painter, Adobe Premiere Pro and Adobe After Effects, along with C#, Python, HTML, CSS, PHP and JavaScript as core development languages, and GitHub for version control. I also have experience integrating applications into VR headsets, with a focus on Oculus Quest, and building AR systems using MRTK2.
