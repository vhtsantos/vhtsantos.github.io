Scrap Heads: The Clock is Ticking

Producer, Animator, Modeler

Short Description

This short film was a personal project that I produced and directed with the intention of participating in the 2023 Made in Deca, a special event organized by the University of Aveiro to showcase student projects.  In the course of this project, I undertook the task of creating the characters from the ground up, crafting their models, textures, and preparing them for animation. Furthermore, I took charge of generating the majority of the assets employed in the film, as well as implementing all the special effects. Upon completing the animation process, I dedicated myself to editing the final product and adding the sound effects. I was assisted by my colleague in the creation of the argument for this piece. This animation was awarded as the Best Animation on Made in Deca 2023.
