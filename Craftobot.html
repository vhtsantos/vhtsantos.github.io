Craftobot VR

Developer, Modeler, Lighting

Short Description

This project is a Virtual Reality game carried out in partnership with Fábrica da Ciência Viva in Aveiro, which simulates a stylized robot laboratory, allowing the player to explore a small range of robotic modules and test how they come together to form a robot, and then have the chance to control them in a playground. In this specific project, I worked on the modeling of the 3D environment's structure and props, as well as all the programming aspects. I developed various skills related to game development in virtual reality, as well as gained a lot of experience.
